About Me

Hi, I’m Fathima Hana — a curious coder, passionate builder, and lifelong learner with a soft spot for clean code and creative problem-solving. I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Applications from VIT Chennai, and a B.Sc in Computer Science, but my love for tech goes far beyond classrooms and certificates.

I’ve always been fascinated by how things work — and even more by how they could work better. Whether it's detecting defects in metal structures using YOLOv8 and OpenCV, predicting sales trends with Python and Linear Regression, or building a line-following, obstacle-avoiding robot with Arduino, I love diving into problems and coming out with working, thoughtful solutions.

Alongside tech, I’ve spent years teaching robotics and programming to students from around the world — an experience that taught me patience, clarity, and just how rewarding it is to see someone else light up when they “get it.”

I’ve had a few proud moments along the way — winning solo coding competitions, scoring top ranks in national entrance exams, and always pushing myself to learn more than I knew yesterday. But more than achievements, what drives me is the journey of growing — building things that matter, one line of code at a time.

Outside the screen, you'll find me exploring self-help books, refining my daily habits, or quietly working on becoming just a little better than I was the day before.

I’m currently looking for software development opportunities where I can bring my skills, creativity, and heart to the table. Let’s build something great.
